Our AI-Enabled Cleantech Edge

EcoPlast is a community-based micro-recycling unit that uses AI to collect, sort, and transform low-value plastic into high-demand products.

Our model is unique because it combines social incentive with technological precision. Unlike large-scale recyclers, we focus on localization, purity, and efficiency by deploying Computer Vision for quality control, guaranteeing material standards and unlocking premium revenue.

See the Detailed Business Model

AI Waste Prediction & Route Optimization

Optimizes collector routes based on predicted accumulation, cutting time and fuel costs by 20-30%.

Computer Vision for Material QC

Automated sorting achieves >75% accuracy in verifying plastic type and contamination, reducing material rejection.

Plastic-for-Reward System

Our 'Eco-Points' app incentivizes consistent raw material supply from households, ensuring a reliable input stream.
